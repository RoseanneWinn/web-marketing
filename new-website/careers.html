Position: Inside Sales Representative

To generate revenue for Izenda by gaining an understanding of customer needs and requirements, and then providing the customer with the appropriate solution, leading to a closed sale.

In your job you need to be outgoing, conscientious, persisitant, diligent, and display the highest degree of integrity. Customers become part of the Izenda extended family and it is your job to increase our family size by making friends, developing trust and closing sales.

Reporting

This position reports to the Chief Sales Officer.

Izenda Background

Izenda is a rapidly growing software technology company in Atlanta. Our growth has exceeded 50% growth each year, for the last three years. It is an excellent time to join our team. Our Ad Hoc Reporting and Dashboard product is being used throughout the United Sates, Canada and Europe. Izenda is the market leader in Embedded Analytics for business applications built on the Microsoft platform. The privately held Atlanta-based software company has delivered ad-hoc reporting, real-time dashboards and data visualization to over one million users by embedding into over one thousand applications. Founded in 2002, the company applies agile software principles and HTML5 technology to give organizations better insight into their data.

Compensation

This is salary plus commission position. On Target Earnings (OTE) is $100k+ in first 12 months.

Experience preferred

  • At least one year successful selling experience in B2B market. Preference will be given to candidates with technology and software sales experience.
  • A four year college degree.
  • Ability to demonstrate and illustrate previous sales processes that led to a closed sale.

Responsibilities

  • Achieving monthly sales quota an average of 75% of the time.
  • Contacting a lead within five minutes, or ASAP, from the time a lead comes in during normal business hours.
  • Continuing to educate yourself. You should always be involved in a new book or audio series on selling. To be the best, you need to study and practice.
  • Insure your prospect and/or customer receives the correct information, literature, etc. What is included in what the customer or prospects receives, is your final responsibility.
  • To do whatever you told a customer or a prospect what you said you would do.
  • To be honest. Lying or misleading is grounds for immediate dismissal.
  • Have a firm understanding of the content in the Izenda Sales Playbook.
  • Know our SalesForce.com system's functionality well enough to:
    • Enter prospects into the database with all the correct fields filled in.
    • Forecast a sale.
    • Produce the sales forecast report.
    • Mail merge and email a client a quote or requested information.
    • Enter a detailed note every time you speak with a customer. Entering a detailed note after every prospect or customer interaction is a job requirement.

As a professional Sales Person, you are required to:

  • Keep a good attitude
  • Have a business attitude in addtion to a sales attitude
  • Have a desire to get better and and continue learn
  • Maintain a desire to be better than the competition
  • Make a guaranteed number of QUALITY calls every business day
  • Keep documentation for every call
  • Maintain the highest degree of integrity
  • Be thinking about getting customers instead of deals
  • Have an accurate forecast available at all times
  • Learn, practice or drill a sales technique every day
  • Learn to really listen
  • Become your customer's trusted advisor
  • Know your product inside and out. And how it compares to the competition
  • View yourself as equal status to the customer
  • Know where to draw the line in the sand
  • Have integrity
  • Be punctual
  • Have and continue to refine a process
  • Get testimonials
  • Follow through as promised
  • Help customer navigate through our company's procedures
  • Understand a customer's business
  • Learn how customers buy versus how we sell
