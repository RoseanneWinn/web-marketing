Clients Type


Companies that build and sell their own branded software products choose Izenda Reports for four principal reasons.
  • Faster time to market with new releases - no long, tedious report writing with traditional programming tools.
  • Cost savings - more "junior" developers can be employed to build any required "standard reports"; they don't need to know Crystal, SQL Reporting Services or other tools.
  • True private labeling - Izenda Reports inherits the look/feel, navigation and security of the core application.
  • Buy vs. Build - The combination of Izenda Reports' powerful feature set and its aggressive pricing model makes the "build in-house" option a far more costly and labor intensive path for the vast majority of software ISV's.
Corporate IT departments, most often staffed with highly skilled developers and database experts, find Izenda Reports a quick and effective solution to reporting services at both departmental and enterprise levels. Izenda Reports is being used to increase productivity and provide on-demand BI to users throughout North America, in Europe, Australia and where ever self-service reporting can make an organization more efficient.
Solution Providers deliver the software equivalent of the Swiss Army knife for business -- they're able to solve virtually any problem. Custom developers have incorporated Izenda Reports into their projects and enjoyed strong positive reaction from their clients. Ad hoc, self-service reporting is here to stay, and users are coming to expect nothing less from their development partners.
