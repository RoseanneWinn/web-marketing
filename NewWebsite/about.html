Izenda is the market leader in Embedded Analytics for business applications built on the Microsoft platform. The privately held Atlanta-based software company has delivered ad-hoc reporting, real-time dashboards and data visualization to over one million users by embedding into over one thousand applications. Founded in 2002, the company applies agile software principles and HTML5 technology to give organizations better insight into their data.

History

Like many great companies, Izenda started with humble beginnings. The company was founded in a Georgia State University dorm room during one of the most significant downturns in the industry's history. Using agile methodologies and software components, Izenda started providing sophisticated solutions to government and non-profit customers with tight budgets and uncertain futures. As the economy recovered, Izenda was perfectly positioned for aggressive growth with its lean structure and flexible platform and started delivering browser based business intelligence tools for medium-sized customers.

After frustrations with dealing with leading reporting solutions, Izenda made a big bet that changed the company. In 2004, Izenda started developing the world's first web-based reporting solution that used AJAX to deliver a highly responsive user experience.

After dozens of successful deployments, the company decided to launch its Reports platform to the world in 2006. Izenda is now working on several enhancements, complementary products and higher levels of integration.

Mission and Values

Izenda's mission is to create agile business intelligence tools that empower ordinary people to make better business decisions. We believe in the power of utilizing the power of metadata to create technology that takes hours or days rather than months or years to deploy. At Izenda, we define software as the experiences we create for our customers and not just the features in our products.
