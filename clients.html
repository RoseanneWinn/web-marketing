ZeeWise

The ZeeWise Zee360 software delivers ad hoc Business Intelligence and Performance Management solutions for franchisors and franchisees. Zee360 provides automated QuickBooks data collection, centralized data analysis, and real time Dashboards with metrics, KPI's and general reporting to help franchisees understand their business better. As the leading provider of Franchise Business Intelligence solutions, this implementation of Izenda Reports enhanced ZeeWise products capabilities by expanding the speed and flexibility by which their users could produce reports -- all without having to invest months or years to develop the same capability in-house. Build or buy? It was clear Izenda had a solution that could be integrated and deployed quickly, leaving the development team to focus on their core product.

eConsulting Network

eConsulting Network produces JobBlox, a web-based job management system for creating and maintaining an organization's job documentation. JobBlox makes developing and maintaining accurate, up-to-date job documentation easier and more efficient. JobBlox is a self-service application that allows managers to submit newly created jobs and to submit changes to existing jobs. With the integration of Izenda Reports, self-service reporting is extended to every user, according to the security and permissions Izenda inherits from the application.

Education Dynamics

Education Dynamics provides marketing and student affairs professionals the tools they need to meet their critical objectives in recruitment, enrollment, yield and retention. The company has pulled together the best in content resources and proprietary technology, all managed by a team of professionals with unparalleled experience in the higher education field. Collectively, it is the only organization that delivers products and services with a proven track record for helping schools find, enroll and retain students. This unique implementation of Izenda Reports lets users create and customize reports from a tree structure - intuitively and quickly.

Independent Software Vendors / OEM's

Companies that build and sell their own branded software products choose Izenda Reports for four principal reasons.

  • Faster time to market with new releases - no long, tedious report writing with traditional programming tools.
  • Cost savings - more "junior" developers can be employed to build any required "standard reports"; they don't need to know Crystal, SQL Reporting Services or other tools.
  • True private labeling - Izenda Reports inherits the look/feel, navigation and security of the core application.
  • Buy vs. Build - The combination of Izenda Reports' powerful feature set and its aggressive pricing model makes the "build in-house" option a far more costly and labor intensive path for the vast majority of software ISV's.
